## Main rule

Do not sell yourself as a generic Product Manager on Upwork initially.

Sell a narrower, easier-to-buy outcome:

> I turn messy workflows or AI product ideas into clear systems, automations, PRDs, user stories, and MVP execution plans.

| Target | Goal |
|---|---|
| Profile | Published with 3 portfolio cards |
| Applications | 10-15 highly targeted proposals |
| First gig type | Small fixed-price, 1-3 day delivery |
| First price range | $35-$150 depending on scope |
| Main goal | First review + proof, not maximum money |

---

## Best first gig filters

Apply when at least 3 are true:

- The job mentions Make.com, Zapier, Google Sheets, Gmail, ChatGPT, workflow automation, PRD, user stories, or AI SaaS.
- The client describes a specific manual workflow or product idea.
- The budget is small but realistic.
- The scope can be delivered in 1-5 days.
- The project can become a portfolio proof point.

Skip when:

- The client wants a full SaaS app end-to-end.
- The job requires deep ML/model training.
- The description is vague and huge.
- There are already 50+ proposals unless the match is extremely strong.
