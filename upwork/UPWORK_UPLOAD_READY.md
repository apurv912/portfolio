# 3. Portfolio Card 1

## Project Title
AI Job Search Automation Engine — Gmail to AI Scoring to CV Pack Workflow

## Category
Automation / AI Workflow / Product Operations

## Project Description
I designed an AI-assisted workflow that ingests job alerts from Gmail, filters and scores them using a structured product-fit rubric, and generates tailored CV packs through a low-cost automation setup.

The problem was simple: job alerts arrive from multiple sources in inconsistent formats, and manually reviewing, scoring, tracking, and tailoring CVs is slow and repetitive.

The solution was a local-first automation pipeline that connects Gmail inputs to structured job scoring, Google Sheets tracking, and CV pack generation. The system uses deterministic filters before AI calls to reduce cost and improve reliability.

Tools used:
Gmail API, Google Sheets, Google Drive, n8n, Claude/LLM prompts, Python, Flask, python-docx, structured scoring rubric.

Business value:
- Converts scattered email alerts into a structured tracker
- Reduces manual review effort
- Uses AI only where useful and keeps cost low
- Creates a repeatable decision system for prioritization
- Produces usable output instead of just analysis

What this proves:
I can take a messy business workflow, define the required data fields, design the process, build a lightweight automation, and create a practical operating system for decision-making.

# 5. Portfolio Card 3

## Project Title
RAG Evaluation & Safety Dashboard — AI Product Quality Project

## Category
AI Product Management / RAG Evaluation / Chatbot Audit

## Project Description
I created a PM-first RAG project that evaluates retrieval quality, citation validity, fallback behavior, latency, telemetry, and safety guardrails.

The problem: many AI chatbot or RAG demos appear functional but do not have evaluation, observability, citation checks, fallback handling, or safety testing. This makes it difficult to know whether the product is reliable enough for real users.

The solution: a RAG microbuild ladder with multiple iterations, including baseline RAG, caching, citation validation, reranking, evaluation scorecards, telemetry dashboard, and safety guardrails.

Tools used:
Python, Streamlit, RAG pipeline, retrieval evaluation, telemetry logs, citation validation, safety testing, product scorecards.

Business value:
- Makes AI chatbot quality measurable
- Helps compare retrieval changes before and after
- Surfaces latency, fallback, reliability, and grounding risks
- Adds PM-level thinking around trust, safety, and product readiness

What this proves:
I understand AI product quality beyond demos: evaluation, trust, citations, safety, observability, and user-facing reliability.
